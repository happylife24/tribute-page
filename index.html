BTS

South Korean boy band

History  of   BTS:

2013   -   debuted with a single album, 2 COOL 4 SKOOL


2014   -   nominated for the top spot as a song, Boy in Luv


2015   -   won first place for the first time with an album, 화양연화 hwa yang yeon hwa


2016   -   gained huge popularity and won the grand prize by an album, WINGS


2017   -   entered the US Billboard Hot 100 chart with an album, LOVE YOURSELF


2020   -   became the first Korean singer to reach number one on the Billboard HOT 100 with an album, Dynamite


2023   -   all members are active worldwide, releasing individual albums or collaborating with singers from other countries
